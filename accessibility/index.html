Universal Design

The Web Accessibility Initiative, or WAI, is the foundation of making content accessible on the Web. Let's take a look at its main points!

Regions

Let's say you've got content you want to present like this:

All About Dogs
There are at least three different kinds of dogs - let's read about them.
Dog Types
• Large
• Small
• Other

[... more content ...]

It's not particularly helpful if it's not organized. Stripped of any visual styling like spacing, font weight, and font size, it'll read like this:

All About Dogs There are at least three different kinds of dogs - let's read about them. Dog Types • Large • Small • Other

That's already jumbled together and confusing, and it'd be even worse if it was longer.
